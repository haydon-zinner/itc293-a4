MindfulMood App

This app was a submission from my Android Development class which recieved a Distinction.

This was an individual assesment that involved completing all the stages of the software development life cycle by indentifying the use cases, the business rule, developing the requirement model along with sequence and activity diagrams, wire frames, and producing a project proposal.

Project Proposal

The app was created to be used as a tool to help mental health professionals decide treatment for patients. By recording the patients moods throughout the day along with the circumstances about why they feel that way, and other external factors such as food intake and sleep, mental health professionals can access if the patients would benefit from medication or lifestyle changes.
